My Work

Analytics

AI & the Manosphere

How do different AI systems respond to coded and explicit misogynistic rhetoric drawn from the manosphere, and to what extent do these systems reinforce, neutralize, or challenge these narratives depending on the prompting persona? This project explores how various LLMs respond to differing rhetorical framings and user identities.

Sleeping in Seattle

This analysis targets trends in tourism in Seattle through Airbnb and uses machine learning to predict where the most profitable Airbnbs will populate in the future.

Birth Control

Uses data on birth control to analyze the popularity and accessibility of different forms of contraception over time with R. The intention of this analysis is to study trends over time in response to social stigmas and cultural norms.

Design

Dear Blank,

'Dear Blank' reinvents analog postage traditions as a secure platform for Gen-Z users in order to foster global community and end the loneliness epidemic. Created during the WINFO Hackathon 2025, our website allows users to send customizable digital postcards without having to share their addresses online.

Manual Wheelchairs

Redesign of non-electric wheelchairs that includes breathable nylon mesh, weight balancing, and a one-way brake that would allow wheelchair users to ascend steep slopes more efficiently.

D3 Research Modules

The Decisions During Drinking (D3) study at UW aims to help young adults have a positive experiences if they decide to drink. I led wireframing and prototyping design using Figma to transform collected data into eight modules distributed to participants.

UW Wrapped

Aims to create a music-sharing website specifically for University of Washington students, designed to foster a community of music lovers. The website would provide a platform where students can easily discover new music, share their current favorites, and connect with peers who share similar tastes. Uses Firebase, Node.js, React.
